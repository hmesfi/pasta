About Audrey's

Audrey's Pasta Shop came to fruition in 1999, bringing lots of love and linguini (and other shapes of pasta) into the heart of Somerville, MA. After all of these years, we still make the most fresh pasta in the whole state of Massachussets, as well as provide our customers with the tastiest sandwiches, sauciest sauces, and the most perfect prepared dishes for your convenience.
